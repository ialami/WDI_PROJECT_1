WDI Project 1 - Ping Pong

This is a ping-pong game with 2 players: player 1 on the left and player 2 on the right. The first to 11 points wins. Player 1 presses Q and A to respectively go up and down. Player 2 presses on up and down arrows to respectively go up and down. Press play to start game.
